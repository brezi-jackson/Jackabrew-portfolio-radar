The Story

We wanted a machine that didn't exist. So we made it.

bre:zi was born in Causeway Bay, Hong Kong. Founders Jackson Hung and Graves Ho were serious about cold brew — and frustrated that no machine matched how serious coffee people actually brew. Precise. Fast. No compromise.

So they built one. With a team from DJI, Sony, and Tesla, they engineered PrecisionBrew™ — cold brew in ~12 minutes, no ice required, 67% less acidic than hot coffee. Not a hack. A system.
