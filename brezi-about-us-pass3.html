The Story

A better way to cold brew.

Brezi started as a hobby. A machine for people who take cold brew seriously — without the 24-hour wait.

We built it in Hong Kong because we couldn't find it anywhere else. We obsessed over the science: extraction pressure, temperature, flow rate. We made it take 10 minutes instead of 24 hours. We made it taste better.

What began as one engineer's experiment is now trusted by world champions and specialty cafés across the globe.

"Brezi started as a hobby. Now it's a ritual we share with you."
