**Summary of the critique:**

Picasso delivered on all three Round 1 non-negotiables — Why Today exists and is the best screen in the app, Corvus data is corrected, history card is complete. The app crossed the line from fitness-app-with-coffee to coffee-brand-with-health-engine.

**Scores by question:**
1. **Palette transition** — Nailed. Typewriter + crossfade + CTA delay is the right pacing.
2. **Bean cards** — Strong. Three distinct visual signatures from one component.
3. **Why Today** — Landing. Copy register is correct. "Pick your weapon" is brand voice.
4. **Flavor visualization** — Under 3 seconds. Gradients are intuitive.
5. **History card** — Personal and warm. "It holds up" is the best insight line.
6. **Marketplace** — Functional but 66% of beans have no price at launch.
7. **Overall** — Coffee brand that happens to be smart. Why Today is the inflection point.

**Top 3 fixes for next round:**

3. Make typewriter timing configurable, not hardcoded — future-proof the hero brand moment
