## The 5 Brew Profiles

### 1. Voltage
*For: Peak recovery, high HRV, time to perform*
Your system is primed. Voltage is Brezi's highest-output profile — concentrated cold extraction with an extended steep that maximizes caffeine clarity without the spike-and-crash. This is what you brew when you mean business.

### 2. Tide Pool
*For: Low recovery, HRV dip, active rest days*
Named for the most productive ecosystem that never looks like it's trying. Tide Pool is smooth, lower-concentration cold brew — gentle extraction, naturally sweeter notes. You're not skipping coffee. You're adjusting the dial.

### 3. First Light
*For: Moderate recovery, weekday mornings, maintenance mode*
Not a recovery day. Not a peak day. Just a morning that needs to begin correctly. First Light is the everyday standard — balanced extraction, clean finish, consistent. Think of it as your baseline, set high.

### 4. Deep Work
*For: Mid-range HRV, focus blocks, sustained cognitive output*
Brewed longer, steeped colder. Deep Work extends the extraction curve for a slower-release caffeine profile that pairs better with 3-hour focus blocks than back-to-back meetings. Not your hardest gear — your smartest one.

### 5. The Blank
*For: Manual override, experimentation, when you just know*
No algorithm. No recommendation engine. Just you, your machine, and a clean extraction profile you can dial in yourself. The Blank is for the days you already know what you need and just want Brezi to execute.

## Brand Guardrails for Dev/Design Handoff
- **Never use:** "optimize your wellness," "fuel your best self," "holistic," "journey," "supercharge"
- **Always use:** specific numbers, active verbs, second person
- **Error states:** direct, never apologetic — "Something broke. Try again." NOT "We're so sorry!"
- **Loading states:** one-line observations — "Cold extraction takes patience. Yours takes 12 minutes."
- **Success states:** earned, not effusive. A checkmark + one dry line beats three exclamation points.

*The app should feel like the machine: precise, fast, no wasted temperature.*
