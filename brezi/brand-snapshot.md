# Brezi Brand Guidelines (from Brandbook 2025 — full PDF)

## Logo
- **Wordmark:** bre:zi — colon between syllables = cold brew time notation
- **Secondary mark:** lowercase "b" in white on black coffee bean oval (for avatars, product engraving)
- **Minimum size:** 120px wide
- **Clear space:** Width of the circle from the "b" all around
- **DO NOT:** stretch, distort, add shadow, swap colon for other punctuation, place on low-contrast backgrounds

- **Dominant:** Black, White, Grey
- **Accent (sparingly):** Blue
- **Correct combos:** White on Black, Black on White/Grey, White on Blue, Blue on Black, Black on Blue, Blue on White
- **DO NOT:** mix colors within a word, use off-palette colors, compromise legibility

## Typography — ABC Social Family
| Style | Usage | Case | Tracking |
|---|---|---|---|
| ABC Social Condensed Bold | Headlines | Sentence case | 0% |
| ABC Social Regular | Body copy | Sentence case | 0% |
| ABC Social Mono Regular | Eyebrow text | ALL CAPS | 20% |
| ABC Social Condensed | CTA Buttons | ALL CAPS | 0% |

- Friendly grotesque with variable font capabilities
- Rounded shapes, dynamic "s", double-storey "a"
- Described as having "a sense of humor"

## Button Style
- Black fill background
- White text, ALL CAPS, ABC Social Condensed
- Example: "ADD TO CART"

## Brand Grammar — The Colon (:)
- The bre:zi colon extends into a wider brand expression language
- Examples: COLD:BREW TIME, EXPRESSO:TIME, IT'S COLD:BREW TIME
- Used with timecode treatments: "07:42AM", "09:35AM" paired with lifestyle imagery
- Tagline: "Anytime can be cold brew time. Anytime can be Brezi time."

## Taglines
- "Brezi. Cold brew coffee anytime in no time."
- "IT'S COLD:BREW TIME"

## Brand Mission & Positioning
- **Mission:** "Brezi is on a mission to bring you a better morning."
- **Goal:** Differentiate from all coffee machine companies, now and in the future
- **Product pillars:**
  - Proprietary instant cooling technology — cold brew in ~5 minutes
  - Ultimate precision in flavor control (light to super strong)
  - Intuitive and innovative design

## Technology Name
- **PrecisionBrew™ Technology**

## Consumer Language
- "Home Brewers" / "Brezi Barista"

## Messaging — Do/Don't
**Do:** Talk human, clear CTA, be innovative, focus on benefits
**Don't:** Overload with tech specs, use marketing buzzwords, sound like Nespresso

## Copy Rules
1. Know who you're talking to — write like you know your best friend
2. Write for readers who scan — concise, no prose walls
3. Make the message crystal clear, especially CTAs
4. Find creative ways to articulate — headlines first, always
5. Educate: tell readers what they need to know, not just what you want to say
6. Be consistent in case/punctuation style across all copy

## Photography Direction
- **Product:** White backdrop, detail shots, iconic moments (drip, frost, ice)
- **Lifestyle:** Joyful, satisfying, always human interaction ("Brezi is a lifestyle, not just an object")
- **Scenarios:** Morning work setup, reading, yoga, pets, relaxed daily moments
- **Aesthetic:** Somewhat industrial yet warm; designer furniture; clothing that echoes brand blue
- **Textures:** Morning sky, sunlight, water, ice, coffee

## Key Design Principle
- Refreshing yet elevated
- Warm but modern
- NOT appliance — lifestyle object on a wellness shelf
