# Brezi — Interview Guide
**Target:** ≥8 qualified interviews, Week 1  
**Channels:** Reddit (r/coffee, r/coldbrewcoffee), Instagram DM, referrals  
**Duration:** 20–30 minutes  

---

## Screener (Before Scheduling)

Ask these before confirming the interview. Do not proceed if they don't qualify.

1. Do you buy cold brew or iced coffee at a café at least 3 times a week?
2. Is your main reason quality/consistency/convenience — or more about the café experience itself?
3. How much do you roughly spend on cold coffee per month?

**Qualifies:** ≥3x/week + function motivation + meaningful spend ($60+/month preferred)  
**Screen out:** experience/social motivation, casual drinkers

---

## Interview Questions

### Opening — Understand current behavior (5 min)
1. Walk me through your typical week with cold coffee. Where do you get it, how often, what do you usually order?
2. What do you like about where you get it from?
3. Is there anything that frustrates you about your current routine?

### Pain mapping (5 min)
4. How much would you estimate you spend on cold coffee in a month?
5. Have you ever thought about making cold brew at home? What happened?
6. What's stopped you from switching or changing anything about this habit?

### Concept introduction (read aloud, no visuals yet)
*"Brezi is a machine that automates cold brew extraction at home. You get café-quality cold brew in [X minutes] — not 12 hours. Same consistency every time. No mess, no waiting."*

7. What's your first reaction to that?
8. Can you describe back to me what you think it does? *(Comprehension check — do not correct, just listen)*
9. Does that solve anything for you specifically?

### Willingness to pay (5 min)
10. If it worked exactly as I described, what would you expect to pay for something like that?
11. *(Reveal actual price)* At [price], what's your reaction?
12. At that price, would you consider buying it? What would push you toward yes or no?

### Switching behavior (5 min)
13. If Brezi worked as described, do you think you'd actually reduce how often you go to the café for cold coffee?
14. What would need to be true for you to use it every day?
15. What would make you stop using it after a week?

### Wrap
16. Is there anything about your cold coffee habit I haven't asked about that feels important?

---

## What to Track Per Interview

| Signal | Notes |
|---|---|
| Comprehension | Did they accurately describe the product unprompted? Y/N + verbatim |
| Pain level | 1–5: how clearly did they articulate frustration with current routine? |
| WTP reaction | Positive / neutral / negative at actual price |
| Switching intent | Would reduce café visits: Yes / Maybe / No |
| Repeat usage potential | Clear daily use case: Yes / Unclear / No |
| Segment fit | Function-driven confirmed: Yes / Borderline / No |
