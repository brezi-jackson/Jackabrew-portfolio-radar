# Brezi — Ad Copy Variants (Week 1–2)
**Segment:** Frequent café cold coffee buyers (function-driven)

---

## Variant 1 — Economic Hook

**Headline:** You're spending $200/month on cold coffee. Here's what it looks like when you stop.

**Body:** Brezi makes café-quality cold brew at home in minutes — not 12 hours. Same consistency. A fraction of the cost. No waiting. No mess.

**CTA:** See how it works →

**Visual direction:** Side-by-side: receipt from café run vs. cost-per-cup at home. Clean, no-nonsense.

---

## Variant 2 — Consistency Hook

**Headline:** Every café cold brew tastes a little different. Brezi doesn't.

**Body:** You know the feeling — some days it's perfect, some days it's off. Brezi brews the same result every time, at home, ready when you are.

**CTA:** Get consistent cold coffee at home →

**Visual direction:** Person drinking cold brew at their desk, no café queue, relaxed. Focus on the ritual at home, not the product hardware.

---

## Variant 3 — Friction Hook

**Headline:** You could make cold brew at home. If it didn't take 12 hours. Now it doesn't.

**Body:** Brezi automated the extraction. You get fresh cold brew whenever you want it — without the overnight wait, the guessing, or the mess.

**CTA:** Stop waiting. Start brewing →

**Visual direction:** Time-lapse or before/after: 12 hours (groan) vs. Brezi process (fast). Keep it punchy.

---

## Landing Page Screener (Before Lead Counts as Valid)

**Shown before price reveal:**

1. "How often do you buy cold coffee or cold brew at a café?"
   - Less than once a week
   - 1–2 times a week
   - 3–4 times a week ✓
   - Daily or more ✓

2. "What's your main reason for getting it there?"
   - It's consistent and high quality ✓
   - It's quick and convenient ✓
   - I like getting out / the café atmosphere ✗
   - It's just habit ← follow up required

3. "How much do you spend on cold coffee per month (rough estimate)?"
   - Under $30
   - $30–$60
   - $60–$120 ✓
   - Over $120 ✓

**Lead counts as qualified signal only if: ≥3x/week + function motivation + ≥$60/month spend**  
All others → tagged "market interest", tracked separately, not counted in segment validation.
